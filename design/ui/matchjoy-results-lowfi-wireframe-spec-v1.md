# MatchJoy Results Low-Fidelity Wireframe Spec v1

> Status: Draft
> Last Updated: 2026-03-30
> Purpose: Define the results overlay wireframe for the iPhone 17 portrait baseline.

## Overlay Slot

Place as a centered modal card within the results overlay zone.

Recommended card:

- centered horizontally
- width: `313-337 pt`
- height: `220-280 pt`
- vertical center slightly above the exact middle

## Internal Layout

### Row 1. Overline

Purpose:

- communicate win / fail state tone quickly

Placement:

- top-center
- small but bold

Content:

- `ORDER COMPLETE` or `ORDER INTERRUPTED`

### Row 2. Badge

Purpose:

- anchor chapter / level context

Placement:

- just below overline
- centered

Content:

- chapter / level tag

### Row 3. Headline

Purpose:

- deliver the main emotional result

Placement:

- centered
- largest text in the card

Content:

- win or fail headline

### Row 4. Performance Summary

Purpose:

- summarize quality of the run

Placement:

- directly under headline
- centered

Content:

- star-band summary
- quality label

### Row 5. Supporting Detail

Purpose:

- explain result in one short readable paragraph

Placement:

- under summary
- centered text block

Content:

- one short explanatory paragraph

### Row 6. Footer

Purpose:

- close the card with progression or retry tone

Placement:

- bottom area of the card
- centered

Content:

- next-step or retry tone line

## Visual Priority

1. headline
2. performance summary
3. supporting detail
4. badge
5. footer
6. overline

## Figma Construction Notes

Use grayscale placeholders for:

- one thin top overline
- one compact centered badge
- one large centered headline block
- one medium summary line
- one medium paragraph block
- one thin footer strip

## Do Not Do

- do not turn this into a full-screen sheet in first pass
- do not let the badge become bigger than the summary
- do not overload the card with too many stacked ribbons or trophies
